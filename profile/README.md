### The Thesis

> You cannot mathematically regulate the latent space of a neural network.
> But you can — and must — regulate the **physics of its execution**.

AI models are becoming commoditized: incredibly smart, cheap, and ubiquitous. But they have *zero* executive authority. AI only **proposes**. Deterministic, mathematically hardened operating systems **govern**.

HELM is the TCP/IP of the autonomous economy — the protocol layer that makes AI agents safe to deploy at scale, with cryptographic proof of every decision.
